# Executive Summary
**Project:** InfraIndex (Global Infrastructure Price Intelligence Platform)

## Overview
InfraIndex is a global price intelligence platform for digital infrastructure, semiconductors, data centers, commodities, and market data.
The first vertical module is **GPU Scanner**, serving as the "Skyscanner for GPU Rentals".

## Objectives
- Aggregate, normalize, validate, and compare GPU rental prices globally.
- Support various compute resources (On-demand, Spot, Reserved, Serverless).
- Expand to CPU, Memory, Storage, Data Center, and Market Data Indices.

## Key Principles
- Production-ready implementation from day one.
- No dummy data; all pricing data must have provenance and validation.
- Clear separation of original and derived metrics.
- Strict legal and data license compliance.
